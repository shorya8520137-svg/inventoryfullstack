# üîê How to Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

## For End Users - Simple Steps

### Step 1: Login to Your Dashboard
1. Go to your inventory dashboard
2. Login with your email and password

### Step 2: Go to Security Settings
1. Look for **"Security"** in the left sidebar (with a lock icon üîí)
2. Click on **"Security"**

### Step 3: Enable 2FA
1. You'll see a section called **"Two-Factor Authentication"**
2. Click the **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"** button

### Step 4: Scan QR Code
1. Download **Google Authenticator** app on your phone:
   - **iPhone:** App Store ‚Üí Search "Google Authenticator"
   - **Android:** Play Store ‚Üí Search "Google Authenticator"

2. Open the app and tap **"+"** to add account
3. Choose **"Scan QR Code"**
4. Point your camera at the QR code on your screen
5. Click **"I've Scanned the Code"**

### Step 5: Verify Setup
1. Look at your Google Authenticator app
2. You'll see a 6-digit code for "hunyhuny"
3. Enter this 6-digit code in the verification box
4. Click **"Verify & Enable"**

### Step 6: Save Backup Codes
1. You'll see 10 backup codes (like: CA1EAEE4, F1228D17, etc.)
2. Click **"Download Backup Codes"** to save them
3. **IMPORTANT:** Keep these codes safe! You can use them if you lose your phone
4. Click **"Complete Setup"**

## ‚úÖ You're Done!

Now when you login:
1. Enter your email and password as usual
2. The page will ask for a 6-digit code
3. Open Google Authenticator and enter the code
4. You're logged in securely!

## üÜò If You Lose Your Phone

1. On the login page, click **"Use backup code instead"**
2. Enter one of your saved backup codes
3. Each backup code can only be used once

## üì± Alternative Apps

Instead of Google Authenticator, you can also use:
- **Authy** (recommended - has cloud backup)
- **Microsoft Authenticator**
- **1Password** (if you have it)

## üîß Troubleshooting

**Problem:** QR code won't scan
- **Solution:** Make sure your phone camera has permission and good lighting

**Problem:** 6-digit code doesn't work
- **Solution:** Make sure your phone's time is correct (Settings ‚Üí Date & Time ‚Üí Automatic)

**Problem:** Lost backup codes
- **Solution:** Contact your system administrator to reset 2FA

## üéØ Why Use 2FA?

- **Extra Security:** Even if someone knows your password, they can't login without your phone
- **Protect Company Data:** Keeps inventory and business information safe
- **Industry Standard:** Most secure companies require 2FA
